Welcome to Stackly

We Are Leading Provider of Transportation & Logistics Solutions

Founded in 2010, Stackly Transportation & Logistics has grown from a small regional carrier to a global logistics powerhouse. We specialize in providing comprehensive supply chain solutions that help businesses move goods efficiently across borders and continents.

Our commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction has earned us the trust of over 500 clients worldwide. We leverage cutting-edge technology and a vast network of partners to deliver seamless logistics experiences.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

We are guided by a clear purpose and strong principles that define who we are and how we serve our clients.

Our Mission

To deliver excellence in transportation and logistics by providing innovative, reliable, and cost-effective solutions that exceed our clients' expectations. We are committed to moving goods safely, sustainably, and efficiently across the globe.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a global leader in logistics and supply chain management, setting industry standards for innovation, sustainability, and customer service. We aspire to connect businesses worldwide through seamless logistics solutions.

Our Journey

Building a Legacy of Excellence

From humble beginnings to global operations, discover the milestones that have shaped our company.

2010

Company Founded

Stackly Transportation & Logistics was established with a single truck and a vision to revolutionize the logistics industry in North America.

2013

First Fleet Expansion

Expanded our fleet to 50 vehicles and opened our first regional distribution center, enabling us to serve clients across 10 states.

2016

International Operations

Launched international freight forwarding services, establishing partnerships with carriers in Europe, Asia, and South America.

2018

Warehousing Division

Opened state-of-the-art warehousing facilities with over 2 million square feet of storage space across strategic locations.

2021

Digital Transformation

Introduced AI-powered logistics management system and real-time tracking platform, revolutionizing our service delivery.

Present

Global Leader

Operating in 50+ countries with a fleet of 500+ vehicles, serving 1500+ clients with comprehensive logistics solutions.
