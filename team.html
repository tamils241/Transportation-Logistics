Our Journey

Leadership Milestones

Key moments that shaped our leadership team and drove Stackly to where it is today.

2009

Company Founded

Robert Anderson founded Stackly Logistics with a single truck and a vision to revolutionize freight transportation across North America.

2013

First International Office

Jennifer Walsh joined as COO and led the expansion into European markets, opening the London office and establishing ocean freight partnerships.

2017

Fleet Reaches 200 Vehicles

Michael Brooks was appointed Fleet Manager, implementing telematics and growing the fleet to 200+ vehicles while maintaining industry-leading safety records.

2021

Warehouse Automation Launch

David Chen spearheaded the $15M warehouse automation initiative, introducing robotic systems and AI-driven inventory management across 8 facilities.

2025

Global Expansion Continues

With 250+ team members across 12 countries, the leadership team continues to drive innovation in sustainable logistics and digital supply chain solutions.
