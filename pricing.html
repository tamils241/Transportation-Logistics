Pricing FAQ

Pricing Questions

Common questions about our pricing plans and billing.

Our pricing is based on a monthly subscription that covers a set number of shipments per plan tier. The Starter plan covers up to 50 shipments, Business up to 200, and Enterprise offers unlimited shipments. Additional shipments beyond the plan limit are billed at discounted per-shipment rates. Exact pricing considers shipment volume, distance, cargo type, and any special handling requirements.

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), ACH bank transfers, wire transfers, and PayPal. Enterprise clients can also set up invoice-based billing with NET 30 or NET 60 terms. All payments are processed securely through our PCI DSS Level 1 compliant payment gateway. You can manage your payment methods through your account dashboard.

Yes! We offer significant volume discounts for businesses shipping more than 500 shipments per month. Discounts start at 5% for 100+ monthly shipments and scale up to 25% for 1,000+ monthly shipments. The yearly billing option saves you an additional 20% on all plans. Enterprise clients receive custom volume-based pricing tailored to their specific shipping patterns and business needs.

You can cancel your subscription at any time with no cancellation fees. Monthly plans remain active until the end of the current billing period. Yearly plans receive a prorated refund for unused months. Individual shipment refunds are available for cancelled or undelivered shipments within 48 hours of the scheduled delivery date. Service credits are issued for any SLA violations on Business and Enterprise plans.
