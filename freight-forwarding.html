How We Work

Our Freight Forwarding Process

A streamlined 6-step process that ensures your cargo moves efficiently from origin to destination with full visibility at every stage.

01

Consultation

We analyze your cargo requirements, trade lanes, and timeline to develop the optimal shipping strategy.

02

Route Planning

Our logistics experts design the most cost-effective and time-efficient routing across all transport modes.

03

Documentation

Preparation and verification of all shipping documents including bills of lading, invoices, and certificates.

04

Customs Clearance

Licensed brokers handle all import/export filings, duties, tariffs, and regulatory compliance requirements.

05

Transportation

Cargo moves via sea, air, or land with GPS tracking, temperature monitoring, and milestone notifications.

06

Final Delivery

Last-mile delivery to the consignee with proof of delivery, cargo inspection, and post-shipment reporting.
