Our Process

How Our Warehousing Works

Our streamlined 6-step process ensures every item is handled with precision from the moment it arrives at our warehouse until it reaches your customer's doorstep.

Step 01

Receive

Goods arrive at our facility where they are scanned, logged into our WMS, and verified against purchase orders. Every item receives a unique identifier for full traceability.

Step 02

Inspect

Comprehensive quality inspection ensures all items meet standards. Products are checked for damage, quantity verification, and proper labeling before acceptance into inventory.

Step 03

Store

Items are strategically placed in optimal locations using our AI-powered slotting algorithm. This maximizes space utilization and minimizes retrieval times for faster order processing.

Step 04

Pick

Orders are efficiently picked using zone, wave, or batch picking strategies. Our robotic systems and trained associates work in tandem to achieve 99.9% pick accuracy.

Step 05

Pack

Items are carefully packed using appropriate materials and methods. Each package is weighed, labeled, and assigned a carrier for shipping with full tracking information.

Step 06

Ship

Packed orders are sorted and loaded for dispatch. We select the optimal carrier and route for cost-effective, timely delivery. Real-time tracking is provided from dispatch to doorstep.

Flexible Storage Solutions

Choose from a range of storage configurations designed to accommodate any product type, volume, or handling requirement with maximum space efficiency.

Selective Pallet Racking

Full access to every pallet position for efficient inventory rotation. Ideal for operations requiring high SKU variety with moderate to high throughput volumes.

Pallet Positions Up to 40ft High

Drive-In Racking

High-density storage maximizing floor space by reducing aisle width. Perfect for bulk storage of similar products with LIFO inventory management requirements.

85% Floor Utilization LIFO Method

Gravity-fed dynamic storage system enabling FIFO inventory rotation. Ideal for perishable goods and high-volume distribution operations with narrow SKU profiles.
