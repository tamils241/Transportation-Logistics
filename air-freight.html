How It Works

Our Air Freight Process

A streamlined 6-step process for seamless air cargo delivery

01

Booking & Reservation

Submit your shipment details through our online portal or contact our air freight specialists. We'll provide instant quotes and reserve cargo space on optimal flights.

02

Cargo Preparation

Our team assists with proper packaging, labeling, and documentation including airway bills, commercial invoices, and customs declarations.

03

Airport Drop & Check-in

Cargo is transported to the airport cargo terminal, undergoes security screening, and is checked in for the scheduled flight with proper weight verification.

04

Air Transit

Your cargo is loaded onto the aircraft and transported to the destination. Track your shipment in real-time through our advanced tracking system.

05

Airport Clearance

Upon arrival, cargo undergoes customs clearance and inspection. Our customs experts handle all regulatory requirements for swift release of your goods.

06

Final Delivery

Cargo is dispatched for final delivery to your specified address. Receive proof of delivery and complete your shipment journey with satisfaction.
